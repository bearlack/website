Why Roof Wash?

Roof washing not only improves the look of your home but also protects and extends the life of your roof. Over time, dirt, mould, algae, and lichen can build up and damage tiles and metals, weaken roof materials, or lead to leaks if left untreated. That’s why we use a gentle soft washing method instead of high-pressure cleaning. This approach safely removes organic growth without risking damage to your roof, tiles, or sealants. As part of every roof wash, we also clean your solar panels at no extra cost to help boost their efficiency and maximise energy savings. Regular roof washing keeps your home looking great, improves energy efficiency, and saves you money on costly repairs down the track.
