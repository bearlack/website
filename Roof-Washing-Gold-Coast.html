Roof Washing and Soft Wash Roof Cleaning

Your roof is one of the most exposed and weather-affected parts of your home — constantly facing sun, rain, moisture and airborne organic growth. Over time, moss, lichen, mould and dirt begin to break down roofing materials, stain surfaces and restrict water flow. Not only does this make your home look tired, it can also shorten the life of your roof and increase the risk of leaks or costly damage. Our professional roof washing service restores your roof safely using a gentle soft wash system, designed to clean thoroughly without the risk of high-pressure damage.

Why Soft Washing Is Better for Roofs
 High-pressure cleaning can chip paint, dislodge tiles, remove protective coatings and force water beneath sheets and flashing — which is why we never pressure wash roofing surfaces. Instead, we apply a controlled low-pressure soft wash treatment, using eco-safe cleaning solutions that dissolve biological growth at the source. 

Our roof washing process:
  1. Pre-wet your roof, and removing any debris like leaves and branches
  2. Apply our eco-safe soft washing mixture, and let that eat away at all mould algae and black mildew
  3. Soft wash rinse to gently remove stains, contamination & organic buildup
  4. Repeat as necessary to remove any stubborn marks or organic growth
  5. Post-treat as needed to slow regrowth & protect the surface for longer

Your roof is left clean, bright and protected — without harsh blasting or unnecessary wear. 

Includes Solar Panel Cleaning — Free with Every Roof Wash 
Solar panels perform best when clean. Dust, dirt, moss and pollen create a film that blocks sunlight and reduces efficiency. As part of every roof wash, we clean solar panels at no additional cost, helping improve performance and maximise energy output. A clean roof + clean panels = better airflow, better efficiency, better energy savings. 

Benefits of Regular Roof Washing
 • Instantly improves street appeal and increases property value
• Removes damaging organic growth before it causes problems
• Prolongs the life of tiles, metals & protective coatings
• Helps prevent leaks, roof decay and premature repairs
• Boosts solar efficiency when panels are included in the clean
• Safe, eco-friendly treatment that protects your home long-term
