Commercial and Body Corperate 

Commercial Pressure washing and Soft Washing
to suit your business

First impressions matter — especially in high-traffic commercial environments. Clean pathways, stain-free concrete and well-maintained exterior surfaces show customers, tenants and staff that your property is cared for and safe to use. 
We provide commercial pressure washing across the Gold Coast and surrounding regions, delivering fast, reliable and high-quality results for businesses, body corporates and facility managers. Whether it’s routine maintenance or a one-off deep clean, we have the equipment and experience to handle large-scale surfaces efficiently. 

What We Clean 
We specialise in restoring and maintaining large external surfaces, including: 
• Carparks & multi-storey parking facilities
• Shopping centres & supermarket fronts
• Strata & body corporate complexes
• Walkways, pathways & high-traffic public areas
• Warehouses, workshops & loading zones
• Commercial buildings, shopfronts & forecourts
• Service stations, drive-throughs & industrial sites
• Schools, sporting clubs & council spaces 
From small retail strips to full commercial complexes — no job is too big. 

Why Businesses Choose Us
 ✔ Commercial-grade equipment for fast large-area coverage
✔ We remove dirt, mould, algae & grime at the root — not just the surface
✔ Degreasers & professional chemicals available for oil, rust & heavy staining
✔ Fully insured for commercial work & public liability
✔ Early morning, night or weekend scheduling available to avoid business disruption
✔ One-off cleans, scheduled maintenance & contracted site care available 
Clean surfaces aren’t just about appearances — they help reduce slip hazards, extend the life of concrete and improve customer experience. 

Eco-Friendly, Safe & WHS Compliant 
We prioritise safe operation at all sites, including traffic control options, hazard management and safe washing practices around pedestrians and vehicles. Where possible, we use biodegradable detergents and controlled wastewater management to protect surrounding environments.
