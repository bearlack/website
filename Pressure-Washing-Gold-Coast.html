Why pressure wash?

Over time, outdoor surfaces collect mould, mildew, grime and stains that regular hosing just can't remove. Pressure washing restores those surfaces back to life, instantly improving the look of your home and boosting street appeal — perfect before guests, property photos, rental inspections or pre-sale preparation.

We provide professional residential pressure washing throughout the Gold Coast, helping homeowners protect and rejuvenate the surfaces that make up their outdoor spaces. Whether it's a quick driveway clean or a full exterior refresh, we ensure a high-quality, even finish with no streaking or patchiness.

Eco-safe treatment for longer-lasting results

Pressure alone only removes what you can see, but mould and algae survive deep within the surface. That's why we use eco-friendly chemical treatments that break down organic growth at its root, not just on the surface.

How we treat your home:

  1. Pre-treat surfaces with biodegradable, eco-safe solutions
  2. High-pressure wash to remove growth, grime & contamination
  3. Post-treat where needed to kill remaining spores and slow regrowth

This means your driveway, paths and outdoor areas stay cleaner for longer, look better between washes, and won't become slippery as quickly. No harsh chemicals, no damage — just a safer, longer-lasting clean.
