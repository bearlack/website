Our Services

Soft Wash House Washing 

We use low pressure cleaning to safely clean the exterior of your home, damage free.
More than just looking pretty, soft washing can extend the life of your home by removing mould and mildew. 

Concrete Pressure Washing 

We Pressure wash concrete driveways, sidewalks, walkways and patios! Our surface cleaners prevent streaks, and chemical post treatment ensures it stays cleaner for longer! 

Damage Free Mildew Removal

We specialise in removing mould and mildew from the exterior of your home. Our patient, damage free process will leave your home looking clean and bright. Some customers say it's like having a new paint job! 

Roof Cleaning 

Soft washing is a gentle way of cleaning roofs without the damage caused by pressure washing. Our cleaning solution targets any growth and discolouration on any roof concrete, terracotta or metal!

About us

GC Shine started with a simple idea, to work outdoors, do honest work, and make a real difference to how a home or business feels. What began as a small weekend side job grew into something bigger, built not through shortcuts or heavy marketing, but through word of mouth, trust, and results that people were happy to recommend. 
From day one, the goal has been the same:
