Our Services

Concrete Pressure Washing

Roof Cleaning 

Soft washing is a gentle way of cleaning roofs without the damage caused by pressure washing. Our cleaning solution targets any growth and discolouration on any roof: colourbond, terracotta or shingles!

About us

GC Shine was started halfway through an engineering degree to make some money on the weekends while working outside doing what I love. Coming from previous jobs pressure washing houses I realised there was a better solution than blasting the walls to kingdom come: a service that is widely used in the US, soft-washing.  

Since learning the techniques soft washing we've helped home-owners restore their homes whilst never damaging a house! Since it's conception, GC Shine has grown into something much larger than I could ever have imagined, but one thing has never changed: my commitment to honest communication through every step of the way; your concerns are understood, discussed and solved, and the outcome is something we're both proud of.

GC Shine, at it's core, is a business that takes pride in it's work. Every home, business and project is treated is as if it were our own. We stand by not only the quality of work we provide, but also the friendly, reliable service that our customers deserve.
