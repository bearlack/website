Why regular window cleaning matters

Living on the coast means salt air, dust and pollen build up on glass faster than most people expect. Left alone, that grime doesn't just look bad — mineral deposits and hard water spots can etch into the glass over time, becoming permanent. A clean window isn't just about the view; it's about protecting the glass itself.

We provide straightforward, reliable window cleaning across the Gold Coast for homeowners and businesses who want a genuinely clear, streak-free finish without the hassle of doing it themselves.

What's included

  • Exterior window cleaning, all accessible levels
  • Interior window cleaning on request
  • Window sills and frames wiped down
  • Cobweb removal around windows and eaves
  • Flyscreens cleaned where they can be safely removed

Our process

  1. Scrub each window to lift dirt, dust and grime from the glass
  2. Squeegee to a clean, streak-free finish
  3. Detail the edges and corners so nothing gets missed
  4. Wipe down sills and frames and clear cobwebs as we go

How often should you clean your windows?

It depends on where you are and how exposed your windows are to the elements. As a general guide: homes closer to the coast or near busy roads tend to need attention every 3 months to stay ahead of salt spray and dust. Homes further inland can often stretch to twice a year. Shopfronts and commercial properties usually look best cleaned monthly, since first impressions matter every day, not just twice a year.
