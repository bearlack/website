House washing the Gold Coast

Soft washing is the safest and most effective way to clean the exterior of your home. Unlike high-pressure blasting, which can damage paint, crack render or force water behind walls, soft washing uses low pressure combined with professional biodegradable detergents to remove dirt, mould and organic growth gently but deeply.

Whether your walls are stained, discoloured or just due for a freshen-up, we restore your home's exterior without risk — leaving surfaces clean, bright and protected for longer.

Professional house washing that won't damage your paintwork

Exterior surfaces are constantly exposed to moisture, dust, salt, road film and organic growth. Regular garden hoses or pressure washers struggle to remove mould at the root, meaning it comes back quickly. Our soft wash method targets growth from the source, not just the surface.

How our soft wash house clean works:

  1. Pre-treat walls with eco-safe cleaning solution
  2. Low-pressure rinse to remove contaminants gently
  3. Post-treat high-risk areas to slow regrowth

The result: a deeper, longer-lasting clean without corrosive chemicals or paint damage.
