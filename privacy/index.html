Privacy

Privacy Policy

GC Shine Pressure Washing ("we", "us") respects your privacy. This page explains, in plain terms, what information we collect and what we do with it.

What we collect

When you submit a quote request through our website, we collect the information you provide: your name, phone number, address, email (if given), the services you're interested in, and any details you add about the job.

How we use it

We use this information only to respond to your enquiry, provide a quote, and deliver the service you've requested. We don't sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Third-party services we use

  • Web3Forms processes and delivers quote form submissions to our email inbox. They only handle the data you submit through the form.
  • Google and Facebook reviews are displayed on our site via a widget that pulls in publicly available review data from those platforms.
  • Website analytics we use a privacy-friendly analytics tool that doesn't use cookies or track individual visitors — it only tells us aggregate numbers like how many people visited and which pages they viewed.
